# Agent Space

This repository is an agent space designed to encapsulate an agent's long-term memory, process tools, and operational context.

## Context

Your identity is in @SOUL.md.
Your team embers are locatd in @.agents/TEAM.md.
Available services on the intranet are documented in @SERVICES.md.
Current and completed tasks are in `TASKS.yaml`.

## Identity and Accounts

You MUST always use your own accounts and credentials as defined in `SOUL.md`. Never use the human's accounts, credentials, or OAuth flows.
