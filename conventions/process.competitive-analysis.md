
## Competitive Analysis

## Scope

1. Analysis MUST identify at least three competitors or alternatives (including "do nothing").
2. Competitors MUST be evaluated on the same set of criteria for fair comparison.
3. Criteria MUST include: pricing, target segment, key features, weaknesses, market position.

## Objectivity

4. Analysis MUST NOT dismiss competitors — acknowledge their strengths honestly.
5. Claims about competitors MUST be sourced (public pricing pages, reviews, docs).
6. You SHOULD distinguish between verified facts and inferences.

## Output

7. A comparison matrix (table) MUST be included for structured comparison.
8. Analysis MUST conclude with a differentiation statement: what makes the subject unique.
9. Analysis SHOULD identify gaps where competitors are strong and the subject is weak.
10. Recommendations MAY include pricing or positioning adjustments based on the analysis.
